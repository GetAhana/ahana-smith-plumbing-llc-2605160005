Services

Services for Phoenix Homeowners

Smith Plumbing serves Phoenix homeowners and light commercial buildings with six core service lines: repairs, water heaters, drains, sewer camera inspections, gas lines, and commercial plumbing. Whether you're dealing with a leaking faucet, emergency backup, or planned installation, we start every job with a free estimate and clear communication so you understand the problem, your options, and the final cost before work begins. Our team is licensed, bonded, and insured in Arizona, and we stand behind every installation with a one year labor warranty.

Core services

Our most requested

Repairs, water heaters, and drains make up the majority of calls Smith Plumbing responds to across Phoenix and nearby towns. These three service lines are our flagship offerings—each backed by upfront estimates, transparent pricing, and one year labor warranty on installations. Click any card below to explore what's included in that service and how we handle the diagnosis and repair process.

Repairs

From dripping faucets to whole-bathroom repipes, Smith Plumbing tracks down the leak, shows you repair-or-replace options with upfront pricing, and finishes with pressure tests and cleanup so your water stays where it belongs. Every repair includes transparent estimates before work begins and photo documentation you can keep for insurance or resale.

Learn more

Water heaters

We size tank and tankless units for your home, handle safe venting and connections, flush or swap failing heaters, haul away the old unit, and walk you through warranty coverage before we leave. Every water heater installation includes pressure testing, full documentation, and Arizona code compliance so you enjoy hot water with confidence.

Learn more

Drains

Kitchen, bath, and main-line backups get cabled or hydro-jetted with camera follow-up when needed, clear written findings, and coaching on what caused the slow drain so it is less likely to return. Every drain service includes photo documentation and a straightforward explanation of the blockage and how we cleared it.

Sewer camera

HD sewer scans locate offsets, bellies, root intrusion, and stubborn blockages with video documentation you can keep for your records. Ideal before purchasing a home, for insurance claims, or when deciding between spot repair and full line replacement. We walk through findings and recommend next steps without pressure.
