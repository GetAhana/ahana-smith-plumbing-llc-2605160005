Services

The services that drive the most calls
in Phoenix

A few of the most common reasons homeowners call us—clear scope, clear next step.

Repairs

From dripping faucets to whole-bathroom repipes, Smith Plumbing tracks down the leak, shows you repair-or-replace options with upfront pricing, and finishes with pressure tests and cleanup so your water stays where it belongs. Every repair includes transparent estimates before work begins and photo documentation you can keep for insurance or resale.

Water heaters

We size tank and tankless units for your home, handle safe venting and connections, flush or swap failing heaters, haul away the old unit, and walk you through warranty coverage before we leave. Every water heater installation includes pressure testing, full documentation, and Arizona code compliance so you enjoy hot water with confidence.

Drains

Kitchen, bath, and main-line backups get cabled or hydro-jetted with camera follow-up when needed, clear written findings, and coaching on what caused the slow drain so it is less likely to return. Every drain service includes photo documentation and a straightforward explanation of the blockage and how we cleared it.

Sewer camera

HD sewer scans locate offsets, bellies, root intrusion, and stubborn blockages with video documentation you can keep for your records. Ideal before purchasing a home, for insurance claims, or when deciding between spot repair and full line replacement. We walk through findings and recommend next steps without pressure.

Gas lines

Stove, dryer, and outdoor appliance gas runs are pressure-tested, leak-checked at every joint and connection, labeled for shutoff locations, and brought up to familiar Arizona code practices so daily operation feels straightforward and safe for your family.

Commercial

Light retail shops and office buildings get documented service scopes, after-hours scheduling when foot traffic matters, and the same cleanup and professionalism we bring to residential jobs so your team can reopen with confidence and zero disruption.

FAQ

Quick answers before you
call

These are the most common questions we get from customers in Phoenix.

Do you offer emergency service?

Yes. We handle burst pipes, water heater failures, and major leaks on a priority basis. Call or text us immediately and we'll give you a realistic arrival window. We try to respond the same day on weekdays and by the next day on weekends.

How fast can you come out?

Call or text us to describe your plumbing issue. We assess urgency and give you a realistic arrival window. For non-emergencies, we typically schedule within a few days. Same-day service is available for urgent leaks and water heater failures.

Do you provide free estimates?

We give you a written estimate before we start any repair or installation. You'll see the price, what's included, and all options so there are no surprises when you pay.
