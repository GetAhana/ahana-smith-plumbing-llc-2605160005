Core plumbing services across Phoenix and surrounding neighborhoods

Smith Plumbing serves Phoenix, Scottsdale, Tempe, Mesa, and surrounding towns with the same reliable, transparent service on every call. Below you'll find our three flagship service lines. Each includes upfront estimates, honest pricing, and one year labor warranty on all installations.

Repairs

Repairs are our most common call—dripping faucets, running toilets, leaking fixtures under sinks, and whole-home repipes. We show up with tools and diagnostic know-how, pinpoint the source, explain your repair-or-replace options with upfront pricing, and finish with pressure tests and cleanup. Most Phoenix and Scottsdale homeowners appreciate our straight talk and transparent estimates before any work begins.

Water heaters

Water heater failures are stressful, especially in the Arizona heat. We size tank or tankless units to match your household needs, handle safe venting and gas or electric connections, haul away the old unit, flush or service the new one, and walk you through warranty coverage and maintenance tips. Same-day emergency service is available for homeowners across Tempe and surrounding towns.

View Water heaters details

Drains

Kitchen drains, bathroom backups, and main-line clogs get cleared with cable or hydro-jetting equipment. When needed, we follow up with sewer camera inspection to confirm the blockage is gone and identify any underlying issues. Mesa and nearby Phoenix neighborhoods benefit from our documented drain service—you receive clear written findings and coaching on how to prevent future slow drains.
